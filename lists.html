• 🐓 One Chicken Breast Fillet
  • 🧀 100g Grated Cheddar Cheese
  • 🍝 One Jar of Pasta Bake Sauce
  • 🫗 An Empty Glass Oven-Proof Dish
  • 🇮🇹 200g of Your Favourite Pasta
  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees celcius.
  2. Place 200g of dry pasta into your oven-proof dish.
  3. Cover in the full jar of pasta bake sauce.
  4. Fill the jar back up with water, and pour into the dish.
  5. Mix together, and bake for 25 minutes in the oven.
  6. Fry off your chicken until golden brown and cooked through.
  7. Remove the tray from the oven. Add in your chicken breast and sprinkle on your cheese.
  8. Bake for a further 20 minutes.
  9. Remove from the oven, leave to sit for 5 minutes, serve and enjoy!
