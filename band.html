My Favourite Band!

You guessed it... Oasis!

Oasis are an English rock band formed in Manchester in 1991. The group initially consisted of Liam Gallagher, Paul "Bonehead" Arthurs, Paul "Guigsy" McGuigan and Tony McCarroll. Liam asked his older brother Noel Gallagher to join as a fifth member a few months later to finalise their formation.
